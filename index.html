Services

Grout Guys provides the following tile and grout restoration services to residents located in Raleigh, Wake Forest, Cary and surrounding areas:

  Caulking/Recaulking

Caulk protects your grout from moisture, extending the life of the tile installation. Recaulking with 100% silicone is the best way to make your home interior look new again.

  Shower Restoration

Shower sealing, caulking, grout removal, grout repair, mold/mildew removal, and soap scum removal.

  Grout Color Sealing

Restore the look of your grout by having it professionally color sealed or stained. The permanent color is applied over your existing grout lines after cleaning. Standard and custom grout colors available.

  Re-Grout

Our grout restoration process involves removing existing grout, cleaning the tile, treating for mold/mildew (if present) and replacing with new grout in all surfaces.

  Grout Clear Sealing

Grout clear sealing protects grout joints from future discoloration without changing the appearance. Our preferred sealant provides maximum stain protection and color preservation in high taffic areas.

  Tile & Grout Cleaning

When your tile & grout become dirty and stained, we have the expertise and equipment to provide a deep and thorough steam cleaning.

  Tile Repair

Loose and hollow tiles don't mean a complete tile replacement. We can secure loose tiles at a fraction of the cost of a total tile replacement.

About Us

Grout Guys LLC is a local family owned & operated small business proudly serving Raleigh, Wake Forest, Cary and surrounding areas.
